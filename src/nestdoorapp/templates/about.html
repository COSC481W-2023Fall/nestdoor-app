The App

A community platform for Eastern Michigan University Eagles to seek help from their flock, such as navigating the "coop" (campus) and their "nest" (department). Our intention was to create a helpful resource for EMU students. This application allows EMU students to ask questions regarding different topics (i.e. department-specific, campus navigation, local recommendations, etc.), and respond to other poster's questions.

Future plans for this app include: a like/dislike feature, use of images and other media in posts and comments, and a Google Maps API implementation.

Our Tech Stack and Development Details

Django is a sophisticated web development framework that boasts comprehensive stack functionalities. Our frontend devs predominantly focus on the construction of views, developing UI, and interactive forms. Conversely, our backend devs center on code to run on the server, specifically within the controllers and models of the framework. Regarding Django's database capabilities, we utilized the default SQLite configurations.
